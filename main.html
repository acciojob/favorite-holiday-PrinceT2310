Goa


Goa is a vibrant coastal state on India’s west coast, known for its golden beaches, Portuguese heritage, lively markets, and relaxed nightlife.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Baga Beach — famous for water sports and nightlife.
  2. Basilica of Bom Jesus — a historic UNESCO site.
  3. Fort Aguada — scenic 17th-century fort with ocean views.
  4. Dudhsagar Falls — stunning waterfall on the Goa–Karnataka border.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market — colorful market for crafts and souvenirs.

Must-Try Foods

  • Goan Fish Curry — tangy coconut-based curry served with rice.
  • Prawn Balchao — spicy pickled prawn dish.
  • Vindaloo — spicy meat curry of Portuguese origin.
  • Sannas — soft steamed rice cakes with curries.
  • Bebinca — traditional Goan layered dessert.
