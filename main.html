Goa


Goa is a vibrant coastal state on India’s west coast, known for its golden beaches, Portuguese heritage, lively markets, and relaxed nightlife. Visitors enjoy water sports, historic churches, scenic forts, and fresh seafood—making it a perfect mix of culture and beach fun.

  1. Baga Beach — famous for water sports and lively nightlife.
  2. Basilica of Bom Jesus — a historic UNESCO site with colonial-era art.
  3. Fort Aguada — scenic 17th-century fort with ocean views.
  4. Dudhsagar Falls — stunning waterfall on the Goa–Karnataka border.
  5. Anjuna Flea Market — colorful market for crafts, clothes, and souvenirs.<

Must-try foods

  • Goan Fish Curry — tangy coconut-based curry served with rice.
  • Prawn Balchao — spicy pickled prawn dish, bold flavors.
  • Vindaloo — spicy meat curry of Portuguese origin.
  • Sannas — soft steamed rice cakes, often served with curries.
  • Bebinca — traditional Goan layered dessert.
